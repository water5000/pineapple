# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary users are agricultural officers and specialists, farm managers, and pineapple growers working with field conditions, crop records, and harvest decisions. Their core workflows are identifying which plots need attention today, forecasting harvests, and recording field data. General users form a secondary audience with report-viewing access only.

## Product Purpose

Smart Pineapple brings plot records, risk monitoring, harvest forecasting, and field observations into one operational dashboard. It helps users intervene earlier, reduce crop losses, produce more reliable harvest forecasts, and maintain more complete field records.

## Positioning

A pineapple-specific decision system that combines AI image analysis with locally relevant crop-risk and harvest intelligence.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, practical, and intelligent. The product should communicate technical confidence in clear, useful language that supports field decisions without making users feel they are operating an AI research console.

## Anti-references

The dashboard should not resemble an overly technical AI console, a generic corporate administration template, or a playful consumer app.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the plots and actions that need attention today.
- Turn forecasts and AI analysis into clear, practical field decisions.
- Keep data entry efficient enough for repeated operational use.
- Make confidence, risk, and data freshness visible without overstating certainty.
- Preserve a clear distinction between operational users and report-only viewers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable Thai typography, complete keyboard support, color-independent status indicators, and reduced-motion alternatives. Keep dense agricultural and model information understandable for users with different levels of technical familiarity.
